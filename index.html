01 / Selected publications

Research that turns
intent into pixels.

My work centers on one question: how can generative models follow complex human intent while preserving everything that should remain untouched?

MULTI-SHOT / 01

Shot-wise keyframe anchors

AAAI 2027 · Under review First author

Unicorn: Controllable Multi-Shot Video Editing Framework via Shot-wise Keyframe Anchors

The first controllable framework for multi-shot video editing, combining shot-aware planning, in-context keyframe editing, and edited-frame anchors. Unicorn improves the strongest baseline by 8.94% on MultiShotBench while preserving cross-shot identity.

  • Video editing
  • Shot localization
  • Cross-shot consistency
Manuscript available after review
SELF-REWARD / 02

One model. One cognitive loop.

ACM MM 2026 · Accepted First author

SREdit: A Self-Rewarding Framework with Intrinsically Aligned Critic for Image Editing

A unified self-rewarding framework that aligns instruction understanding with reward evaluation, reducing semantic drift in reinforcement-learning-based image editing.

  • Image editing
  • Self-rewarding
  • MLLM–DiT
Final paper coming soon
FEDERATED / 03

Generate what each client lacks.

ICML 2026 · Poster First author · Equal contribution

FedPDG: Prediction Discrepancy–Guided Data Generation for Heterogeneous Federated Learning

Uses model prediction discrepancy to discover missing local data and guide diffusion-based generation, improving heterogeneous federated learning by up to 3.82%.

  • Federated learning
  • Diffusion
  • DPO

03 / About

Curious about how we see, think, and create.

I’m a master’s student in Software Engineering at Beihang University, advised by Prof. Jianwei Niu in the LDMC Lab. My research spans diffusion and flow-matching models, multimodal language models, and reinforcement learning for visual generation.

Before graduate school, I completed my bachelor’s degree at Beihang, where I was named an Outstanding Graduate and received national first prizes in mathematical modeling and compiler design.

Outside the lab, I spend time with R&B and hip-hop—drawn to rhythm, texture, and voice—and with philosophy, returning to questions of mind, freedom, meaning, and how to live.
